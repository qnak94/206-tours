Hotel Villa Grace ****
Fra Radovana Petrovića 1, 88266 Međugorje, Bosnia and Herzegovina

About the Hotel Villa Grace:

Hotel Villa Grace is located in Međugorje, 92 m from St. Jacobs Church, and provides free WiFi. Among the various facilities of this property are a terrace, a shared lounge and a bar. Guests can enjoy a selection of local cuisine in the in-house restaurant.

Rooms

At the hotel, each room comes with a desk. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom equipped with a bidet, while certain rooms have a seating area. At Hotel Villa Grace each room includes a closet and a flat-screen TV.

Every item at Villa Grace Međugorje has been selected with the utmost care and love. Our wish is to ensure that every visitor or guest experiences Međugorje in the right manner. We thus provide top-notch services, accommodation with a special and elegant interior design and unique and personalized approach in order to make the stay as pleasant as possible.
